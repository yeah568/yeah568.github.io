Project Launch
Sample, remix, and mashup your favourite music. All in your browser.
GitHub >>

    Buttons can be easily added and removed with the buttons at the top.

    ...or from local files on your computer.
    Additionally, you can bind buttons to keys on your keyboard, making it even easier to control.

the details

A simple Ableton/Novation Launchpad type sampler that lives in your browser. Add tracks from your local computer or SoundCloud, and sample, remix and mashup your favourite music.

Entirely clientside, no server required.

Created at DubHacks 2014 in Seattle, WA from October 17-18 by James Liu, Shikib Mehri, Mike Park and William Qi.
